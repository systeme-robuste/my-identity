title: Getting started
description: Create your first site in 5 minutes.
---

## Prerequisites

- A My Identity account (free, no card required)
- A modern browser
- Optional: a domain name

## Step 1 — Create a site

From the dashboard, click **+ New site**. Give it a name and a slug (e.g. `my-site` → `my-site.myidentity.app`).

## Step 2 — Add pages

Click **Pages** → **+ New page**. Each page has a unique slug (e.g. `index`, `about`, `pricing`).

## Step 3 — Compose with blocks

Open the editor. The left palette has 10 block types: Hero, Text, Image, Gallery, Form, CMS, Embed, Pricing, FAQ, Footer.

Drag-drop a Hero block, double-click to edit the title. All changes are **autosaved**.

## Step 4 — Publish

Click **Publish** in the top right. Your site is live in under 30 seconds, served from the global Cloudflare edge.

## Step 5 — Connect a domain

In **Settings → Domain**, add your domain (`example.com`). Follow the DNS instructions (CNAME to `myidentity.app`).
